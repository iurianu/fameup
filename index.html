Frequently Asked Questions

Your access to FameUp App has been denied if you didn't respect one or many of the following conditions:
  • Your Social Media account must be public;
  • Your engagement has to be 5% (eg. if your profile has 5k followers, and the content posted does not attract more than 10% of the followers displayed, your access to FameUp App will be denied);
  • At least 80% of the pictures on your profile must be with you. (20% of the content can be pictures of landscapes, pets, giveaways etc.)
  • You need to have a minimum number of 3 posts in the last week on the indicated Social Media account;
  • You need to have a minimum of 20 posts on the indicated Social Media account;
  • You can't use images that illustrate intimate sequences from your life on your Social Media account;
  • You can't use images whose quality is poor (not sufficiently stylized) on your Social Media account;
  • You can't use obscene or indecent images, including any violent or pornographic content or containing nudity, violent or explicit sexual material, or representations of violent or sexual acts on your Social Media account;
  • You can't use false, instigating or defamatory or false images on your Social Media account;
  • You must be at least 16 years old.
To take the offer available in the FameUp App you have to accomplish the specific conditions of that offer.
You have access to a single offer at 48h as an Influencer on the FameUp App.
You can put a single free offer as a Brand on the FameUp App.
You can give unlimited offers as a Brand on the FameUp App.
